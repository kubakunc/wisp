Wisp — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Wisp ("the app", "we", "us"), a sleep-sounds app published by VeloLogic Labs, handles information. Wisp does not require an account — you don't give us a name, email, or password to use it.

Short version: Wisp plays ambient sounds. To run, improve, show ads to free users, and manage subscriptions, the app uses Google and RevenueCat services that collect pseudonymous usage, advertising, and purchase data. We do not collect your name or email, and we do not sell your personal data.

1. Information collected

Usage & analytics — Google Analytics for Firebase

  • Which sounds and sound combinations ("mixes") you play and for how long, and whether a sound is a premium-tier sound;
  • Screen views, app version, session/usage events;
  • Device model, operating-system version, language, and coarse region;
  • A pseudonymous Firebase installation identifier.

This data is pseudonymous and is not linked to your name or email.

Advertising — Google AdMob (free tier only)

  • Your device advertising identifier and ad-interaction data;
  • Coarse location derived from IP address, used to serve ads.

In the EEA/UK we ask for your consent before serving personalized ads, and you can choose non-personalized ads. You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings. Premium subscribers do not see ads.

Purchases — Google Play Billing & RevenueCat

  • Your subscription / entitlement status and purchase tokens;
  • A pseudonymous app-user identifier used to restore purchases.

Payments are processed by Google Play — we never see or store your card or payment details.

On your device only

Your saved mixes, downloaded sound files (cache), and settings are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to us.

2. Permissions the app uses

  • Internet — download sounds, serve ads, and send analytics.
  • Foreground service / media playback — keep sounds playing while the app is in the background or the screen is off.
  • Wake lock — prevent the audio from being interrupted during playback.

3. How the data is used

  • To run the app and play your sounds;
  • To understand which sounds/features are used so we can improve Wisp;
  • To show and cap advertising for free users;
  • To manage subscriptions and restore purchases.

4. Sharing

We share data only with the service providers that make the app work (listed below), acting as our processors. We do not sell your personal data.

5. Third-party services & their policies

  • Google (Firebase & AdMob & Play): policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase data handling: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob & advertising: AdMob data disclosure
  • RevenueCat: revenuecat.com/privacy

6. Children

Wisp is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable) and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Your rights

Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR in the EEA/UK), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, and object to certain processing. You can also reset your advertising ID in device settings, and uninstalling the app removes the locally-stored data. To exercise these rights, contact us below.

8. Data retention

Analytics data is retained according to our Firebase configuration (by default up to 14 months). Purchase records are retained as required for accounting and to honor subscriptions.

9. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections and our providers apply industry-standard safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; we'll revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the app or store listing.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: privacy@wisp.app
